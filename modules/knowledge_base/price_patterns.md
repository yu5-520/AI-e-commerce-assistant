# Price Pattern Knowledge

Store reusable pricing structures and tested price bands.

Suggested fields:
- platform
- mode
- category
- cost
- sale_price
- shipping_cost
- gross_margin
- test_price
- coupon_price
- activity_price
- inventory
- conversion_effect
- profit_effect
- refund_risk
- stop_loss_rule

Principle: price knowledge must track both conversion and profit. More orders are not useful if profit or refund risk becomes bad.
