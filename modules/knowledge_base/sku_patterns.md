# SKU Pattern Knowledge

Store SKU structures that improve conversion or profit, not random SKU names.

Suggested fields:
- platform
- category
- sku_role
- sku_structure
- price_ladder
- user_adoption
- conversion_effect
- refund_risk
- applicable_boundary

Common SKU roles:
- low-price traffic SKU
- profit SKU
- bundle SKU
- upgraded material/function SKU
- color/spec expansion SKU
