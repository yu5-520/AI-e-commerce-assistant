# Title Pattern Knowledge

Store reusable title structures, not just single titles.

Suggested fields:
- platform
- mode
- category
- title_type
- keyword_structure
- sample_title
- user_experience_score
- operation_effect_score
- usage_count
- applicable_boundary

Common Pinduoduo title types:
- search coverage: product word + season + function + scenario
- price feeling: product word + low price / clearance / coupon + key benefit
- long-tail: product word + specific attribute + user scenario
